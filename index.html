About the game

Dive into the challenge and unravel the mystery... This is NUMBER HUNT, A game where you have to guess 4 numbers. You can guess by inputing numbers in the provided space. The game will tell you if your guess is right and positioned correctly, or wrong. You keep guessing and listening to the hints the game gives you. You have to figure out the right number. You get clues like how many pots and pans you have. NB: If you get a pot it means your guess is correct and is positioned correctly, if you get a pan it means your guess is correct but is positioned in the wrong box.

This is a game against computer, Where the the computer picks four random numbers in the range 0 - 9. Your aim is to guess the numbers and arrange the numbers accordingly using the hint provided. The hint provided is the range of computer selection (smallest to Largest Number) and most importantly POTS AND PANS. POT means one of the numbers you guessed is correct and placed in the right order, PAN means you guessed a number right but wrongly placed. For example: COMPUTER SELECTS 1-2-3-4 AND YOUR GUESS IS 1-4-2-8. The game response will be 1 POT and 2 PANS because 1 is correct and rightly placed which makes it a POT, 4 and 2 are also correct but wrongly placed which results in PAN.

Crack the code!🧐

  • Players input their guesses by typing the numbers directly.
  • The game generates a random secret number within the specified range, which the player must guess correctly.
  • After each guess, the game provides feedback in the form of clues to help players refine their guesses. Clues may include whether the guess is too high, too low, or if it's the correct number.
  • Players continue to make guesses within the allotted number of attempts, using deduction and logic to narrow down the possibilities.
  • If players successfully guess the secret number within the allowed attempts, they win the game.
